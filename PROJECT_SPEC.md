# ExpiryGO - Product Specification

# Vision

ExpiryGO is an offline-first smart pantry and expiry management application.

Its purpose is to help users:

- Never forget food expiry dates
- Reduce food waste
- Save money
- Organize household inventory
- Receive intelligent suggestions
- Eventually become an AI-powered kitchen assistant
